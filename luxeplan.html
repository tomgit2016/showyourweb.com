Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 21, 2025

Thank you for using LuxePlan ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal information.

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No financial data is collected.
  • No analytics or usage data is collected.
  • No tracking technologies are used.

2. How the App Works

The App is a mobile application for luxury item financial planning. It helps users manage wishlists for high-value assets (Real Estate, Cars, Yachts, Watches, etc.) and provides sophisticated financing calculations with real-time market data context. All calculations are performed locally on your device. Information entered into the App never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services, including analytics tools, advertising networks, or external APIs.

4. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect any data, we do not share data with third parties, sell data, or disclose data for marketing, analytics, or legal purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

No user data is stored on external servers. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of unauthorized access or data breaches involving user information.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App complies with applicable privacy laws such as COPPA and GDPR by design.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to access, correct, or delete.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
