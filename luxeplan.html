LuxePlan: Wishlist Financing

Plan your dream purchases

Instantly estimate monthly payments and see how interest rate changes impact your budget. Plan your dream purchases with live market context.

Available now for iPhone and iPad.

See exactly how interest rates impact your monthly payments.

LuxePlan is the most convenient way to estimate financing for your dream purchases. Whether you're eyeing a luxury watch, a new car, or a home renovation, LuxePlan helps you answer the question: "How much will this actually cost me per month?"

Key Features

  • Interactive Rate Slider: Don't just guess interest rates—visualize them. Slide to instantly see how a 0.5% or 1% rate hike changes your monthly payment.
  • Instant Payment Estimator: Quickly calculate Monthly or Bi-Weekly payments based on price, down payment, and loan term.
  • Market Context: View live market indices (Dow Jones, Nasdaq, S&P 500) and Bond Yields purely as a reference to help gauge the economic climate while you plan.
  • Visual Wishlist: Organize your goals in a beautiful dashboard with custom images and details.
  • Regional Support: Seamlessly toggle between US and Canadian data for relevant interest rate benchmarks.

Stop guessing and start planning. Download LuxePlan to visualize your financial goals with clarity.
